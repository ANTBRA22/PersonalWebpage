Why is Tennis My Favorite Sport?

Some of My Opinions

  • The beauty of tennis is that it is an individual sport. No one besides you can strategize and problem solve.
  • Tennis requires alot out of a person. Good hand-eye coordination, stamina, flexibility, and reflexes are all necessary.
  • It is also a great spectator sport. Tennis brings constant action whether watching in-person or on TV!
